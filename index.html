Happier Than Ever

Released Jan 2021

Musicians:

  • Billie Eilish, Vocals
  • MFinneas O'Connell, producer & Backing vocals

Happier Than Ever is the second studio album by Billie Eilish. It was released on July 30 2021, by Darksoom and Interscope Records. Billie wrote the album with her frequent collaboration, her brother Finneas O'Connell, who produced the album himself. Billie says the COVID-19 pandemic was her biggest inspiration for the album. Eilish has revealed that the creative process of Happier Than Ever felt "very natural", unlike her previous projects where she constantly felt anxious and under pressure. In an interview with Vevo, Eilish said that she wanted to create "a very timeless record", mostly being inspired by jazz singers that she listened to growing up such as Julie London, Peggy Lee and Frank Sinatra.
